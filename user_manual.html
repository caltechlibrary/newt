Newt User Manual

What is in the Newt toolbox?

  • Newt, a development tool that will generate a basic Newt YAML file; allow you to generate SQL, PostgREST configuration and Mustache templates; runs Newt Router and Mustache as one process; can manage PostgREST in another process
  • Newt Router, manages your requests and data pipelines
  • Newt Mustache, a light weight template engine
  • Newt Generator, bootstraps your SQL and Mustache templates (being written)
